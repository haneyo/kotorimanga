Web Browser

Built-in Manga Browser

Browse manga websites directly within the app. Our intelligent detector automatically finds manga images on any page, letting you download entire chapters to your shelf with a single tap.

  • Auto-detect manga images on any page
  • One-tap download to bookshelf
  • Bookmark & share support
Reader

Immersive Reading

Read manga your way with single page, double page, and strip (webtoon) modes. Switch between tap and swipe page turning, with left-to-right or right-to-left reading direction.

  • Single / Double / Strip modes
  • Parallax motion & breathing effects
  • Volume button page turn, auto bookmark

Wi-Fi Manga Transfer

Transfer manga between your computer and device over Wi-Fi. Simply open a browser on any device on the same network to upload manga files, or download manga from your shelf to your computer.

  • Upload manga from any device via browser
  • Export manga from your shelf to any device
  • No cables, accounts, or extra software needed
And More

Thoughtful Details

Reading Progress

Auto-saves your reading position. Continue exactly where you left off, or choose to start from the beginning.

Motion Effects

Parallax motion and breathing effects bring manga pages to life. Powered by on-device Vision segmentation.

Privacy First

All processing happens on-device. No account required, no tracking, no data collection. Your manga, your privacy.

iCloud Sync

Sync your bookshelf and reading progress across all your iOS devices seamlessly via iCloud.

30+ Languages

Interface available in over 30 languages including Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, and many more.

Volume Button Turn

Use hardware volume buttons to flip pages. Perfect for one-handed reading on the go.
