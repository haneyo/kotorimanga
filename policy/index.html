隐私政策

最近更新：2026年6月

Kotori Manga（下称"我们"或"本应用"）是一款本地优先的漫画阅读器应用。我们高度重视并保护用户的隐私。本隐私政策旨在说明本应用如何处理您的数据。

核心原则：零服务器收集
Kotori Manga 不拥有也不运营任何云端服务器，我们不会收集、存储或共享您的任何个人数据、浏览历史、漫画文件或云盘登录凭证。所有数据完全属于您个人，并仅保存在您的本地设备或您选择的云服务商处。

功能与数据交互说明

  • 本地导入与 WiFi 传书： 通过 Files 应用或 WiFi 传输导入的漫画文件，完全离线存储在本应用的安全沙盒目录中。WiFi 传输功能在设备本地创建 HTTP 服务器，所有传输仅在局域网内进行，不经过任何外部服务器。
  • 本地网络存储（WebDAV / SMB / FTP / OPDS）： 本应用允许您连接到您个人的网络服务器以读取或下载漫画。所有连接凭证（密码、URL 等）均经过加密后安全地保存在您的本机设备上。所有操作均为只读模式。
  • 第三方云存储（Dropbox / MEGA / iCloud / Box）： 本应用集成了官方 SDK/API 允许您读取云盘中的漫画。所有登录验证均通过官方 OAuth 安全渠道直连完成。我们不会将您的访问令牌或网盘文件传输给任何第三方。所有操作均为只读模式。
  • 内置网页浏览器与批量下载： 本应用内置 WKWebView 浏览器，用于协助您预览漫画网站并打包下载其中的图片至本地。应用不会追踪、上传或分析您的任何网页浏览历史及 Cookies。
  • iCloud 同步与备份： 本应用利用 Apple iCloud 技术在您个人的 iOS/macOS 设备之间同步书架数据（漫画元数据、文件夹、书签）和应用设置。该数据直接通过苹果公司的安全加密通道传输，我们无法获取。

外部源的只读模式

  • 完全只读模式： 本应用所连接的所有外部网络源——包括但不限于 SMB、WebDAV、OPDS、FTP、Dropbox、MEGA、iCloud、Box——均以严格的只读模式运行。本应用不包含任何能够对外部源进行写入、删除、重命名或以其他方式修改文件的代码。

广告服务

  • 本应用在免费版本的书架页面展示广告。广告由 Google AdMob（中国大陆地区使用本地化广告服务）提供。广告服务商可能会收集匿名设备信息以提供相关广告。详细信息请参阅 Google 的隐私政策。
  • 您可以通过一次性内购永久去除广告，或通过观看激励视频获得 24 小时去广告权限。

数据安全

鉴于所有数据均存储在您的 iOS 安全沙盒内或您的个人云盘中，数据的安全性完全取决于您设备的自身防护以及云服务商的安全保障。我们强烈建议您为设备启用锁屏密码或 Face ID。

隐私政策的变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。所有修订将直接在此官方网页上公布。

联系我们

Kotori Manga ("we", "our", or "the App") is a local-first manga reader application. We respect and protect the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how the App interacts with your data.

Core Principle: Zero Server Collection
Kotori Manga does NOT own or operate any cloud servers to collect, store, or share your personal data, browsing history, comic files, or cloud storage credentials. All data belongs strictly to you and stays on your device or your chosen cloud providers.

Features and Data Interaction

  • Local Import & WiFi Transfer: Comics imported via the Files app or WiFi transfer are stored completely offline within the App's secure sandbox directory. WiFi transfer creates a local HTTP server on your device — all transfers occur over your local network only, never through external servers.
  • Local Network Storage (WebDAV / SMB / FTP / OPDS): The App allows you to connect to your own local network servers to read or download comics. All connection credentials (passwords, URLs) are encrypted and stored locally on your device. All operations are strictly read-only.
  • Third-Party Cloud Storage (Dropbox / MEGA / iCloud / Box): The App integrates official SDKs/APIs to let you read comics from your cloud accounts. Authentication is performed directly via official OAuth securely. We do not transfer your access tokens or cloud files to any third party. All operations are strictly read-only.
  • Built-in Web Browser & Batch Downloader: The App includes a built-in WKWebView browser to help you preview comic websites and download web images as local packages. No browsing history or web cookies are tracked, uploaded, or analyzed.
  • iCloud Sync & Backup: The App utilizes Apple iCloud to sync your bookshelf data (manga metadata, folders, bookmarks) and app settings across your own iOS/macOS devices. This data goes directly through Apple's secure infrastructure; we have no access to it.

Read-Only External Sources

  • Strictly Read-Only Mode: All external network sources linked to the App — including but not limited to SMB, WebDAV, OPDS, FTP, Dropbox, MEGA, iCloud, and Box — operate in a strictly read-only mode. The App contains no code capable of writing, deleting, renaming, or otherwise modifying files on any external source.

Advertising

  • The free version of the App displays advertisements on the bookshelf page. Ads are served by Google AdMob (or a localized fallback ad service for users in mainland China). Ad providers may collect anonymous device information to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • You may remove ads permanently via a one-time in-app purchase, or obtain 24-hour ad-free access by watching a rewarded video.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally inside the iOS secure sandbox or within your personal cloud storage, the security of your data relies on your device security and your cloud provider's protection. We strongly recommend protecting your device with a passcode or Face ID.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this official page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via email.
